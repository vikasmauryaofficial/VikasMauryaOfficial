Who I Am

Educator &
Researcher

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad. My academic journey has been defined by curiosity — from building ML pipelines to publishing Scopus-indexed research on privacy-preserving AI systems.

My M.Tech thesis focused on Efficient Brain Tumor Classification Using Deep Learning, combining YOLOv8, Federated Learning, and Image Segmentation to advance medical AI.

I'm passionate about bridging academic research and real-world application — creating interactive learning experiences that prepare students for the evolving landscape of technology.
